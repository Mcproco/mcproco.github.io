First (Rough) Interview

23 FEB 2026


My first interview was ROUGH. Like, really rough:


"Hi, how are you?"

"Good, how are you?"

"Fine, how are you?"

"What?"


In short, not great. For some strange reason, I couldn't critically think during that 1 hour session.

All of my responses were automated and pre-written, making for a very unauthentic and awkward experience. Don't get me wrong, the information he gave was really useful.

But the fact that he would say "you know" at least once in each sentence, and the fact that I had just got home from a 3 hour car ride did not make it any better. I couldn't even expand and respond upon the information he was giving.


It was rough, rough and nothing short of rough.
